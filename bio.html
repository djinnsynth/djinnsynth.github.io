An alias for all digital creations by me, Aaron Louis Carter, djinnsynth is the name I've given to my creative self.

This site showcases the most positive aspects of living with schizophrenia, the ability to construct high quality abstract art!

I do not, nor will I ever have, a list of qualifications, but the contents of this site represent my ability to learn systems and frameworks to tear things apart to piece them back together to create something else.
