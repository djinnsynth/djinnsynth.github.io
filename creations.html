The way I make my art is sometimes tricky to explain, but it all boils down to abstracting from the source material as much as possible, which in most cases is medical imagery.

Once I zoom into a scan and find a good clump of pixels, I use image editing software to filter and cut out pieces that I like.

After I get some results I like, I will start to blend them together to eventually find my way to a finished product.
